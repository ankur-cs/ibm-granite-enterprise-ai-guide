# Introduction to IBM Granite

## Overview

**IBM Granite** is a family of **enterprise-focused foundation models** developed by **IBM** to help organizations build, deploy, and scale **trustworthy Generative AI solutions**.

Unlike general-purpose AI models, **IBM Granite** is designed with enterprise requirements in mind, including:

- **Security**
- **Governance**
- **Transparency**
- **Reliability**
- **Business Productivity**

IBM Granite is part of the **IBM watsonx** portfolio and supports organizations in building enterprise-grade AI applications.

---

## What is IBM Granite?

### Definition

**IBM Granite** is IBM's family of **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **Foundation Models** built specifically for enterprise Artificial Intelligence.

### IBM Granite helps organizations:

- Build **AI Assistants**
- Generate **Business Content**
- Automate **Knowledge Management**
- Improve **Customer Support**
- Enhance **Software Development**
- Accelerate **Digital Transformation**

## Key Features of IBM Granite

### **Enterprise-Ready Foundation Models**

Purpose-built for enterprise business applications.

### **Generative AI**

Supports intelligent text generation, summarization, coding assistance, and conversational AI.

### **Responsible AI**

Built using IBM's principles for trustworthy and transparent AI.

### **AI Governance**

Integrates with **IBM watsonx.governance** to help organizations manage AI responsibly.

### **Hybrid Cloud Support**

Supports deployment across public cloud, private cloud, Hybrid Cloud, and on-premises environments.

### **Enterprise Security**

Designed to support enterprise security, privacy, and compliance requirements.

### **Scalability**

Capable of supporting enterprise-scale AI workloads.
