## 📖 About IBM Granite

**IBM Granite** is IBM's family of enterprise-focused Foundation Models designed to power Generative AI applications across industries.

These models are built specifically for:

- Enterprise Artificial Intelligence
- Generative AI Applications
- Business Automation
- Intelligent Decision Making
- Software Development Assistance
- Knowledge Management
- Responsible AI Deployment
- Enterprise Transformation

IBM Granite is integrated into the **IBM watsonx platform**, providing organizations with a complete ecosystem for building, deploying, and governing AI solutions.
